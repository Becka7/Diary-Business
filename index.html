About us

Fresh Dairy is a locally owned, locally focused dairy enterprise that is founded on our desire to provide affordable, nutritious and enjoyable dairy products. We are passionate about the experience we deliver every day and we want to breathe new life and energy into the industry as a whole.This is a small-scale family farm that was started in 2007 in Tala, Machakos County, Kenya .It has four shades each with 30 dairy cows and an extra shade for steers. The farm specializes in breeding of pedigree Holstein Friesian and Jersey cows. In an effort to breed the best, we artificially inseminate our cows with imported semen from North America ( CRI ) and Netherlands ( CRV )and New Zealand.
